## Visual Identity

### Brand Evolution

The LHFS visual identity has evolved from its warm, heritage-driven roots into a sharper, more luminous expression of the same core character. The update does not abandon who we are — it sharpens it.

**Before:** Warm white surfaces, classic serif typography, traditional document-style layouts.
**Now:** Deep navy and charcoal foundations, amber gold as a glowing accent, frosted glass surfaces, clean sans-serif with spaced all-caps labels. The result feels like the same trusted institution seen through a cleaner lens.

### Logo

The LHFS logo remains unchanged — it is the heritage anchor.

- **Wordmark:** "Land Home" in a classic serif script, with "FINANCIAL SERVICES, INC." in small all-caps beneath.
- **Icon:** A warm amber/gold house nestled between "Land" and "Home" — the brand's most recognizable mark.
- **Feel:** Heritage, reliability, and warmth. The logo does not need to modernize — the surrounding system does.
- **Usage:** Always maintain clear space. Do not alter colors, stretch, or apply visual filters. On dark surfaces, use the full-color version.

### Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| **Background — Dark** | Deep Field | `#07112B` → `#1C1712` gradient |
| **Brand Accent — Primary** | Amber Gold | `#C4902A` |
| **Brand Accent — Glow** | Gold Light | `#E8B84B` |
| **Supporting Depth** | Navy Depth | `#0D2045` |
| **Text / Light Surfaces** | Warm White | `#F8F5F0` |
| **UI Surface** | Glass | `rgba(255,255,255, 7%)` with backdrop blur |

**Amber Gold** remains the soul of the brand — the house icon, the call to action, the earned accent. Placed against deep navy and charcoal, it glows with presence. **Glass surfaces** (backdrop-filter blur, semi-transparent) replace flat white panels — translucent, layered, and alive.

### Typography

| Role | Style | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Logo / Heritage** | Classic serif or script | Logo only — preserve as-is |
| **Headlines** | Bold sans-serif, tight tracking | Section titles, hero copy, campaign statements |
| **Body Copy** | Clean, readable sans-serif | All communications — web, print, email |
| **Labels / Eyebrows** | All-caps, wide-spaced sans-serif | 0.20em+ letter-spacing, used for section markers and UI labels |
| **Data / Figures** | Tabular numeric sans-serif | Rates, stats, loan parameters — legibility first |

### Imagery

- Real families, couples, and individuals in genuine home settings.
- Warm, natural lighting — inviting and authentic.
- Diverse representation across all audiences.
- All home types: traditional, manufactured, renovated, new construction.
- On dark digital surfaces: imagery should feel cinematic and grounded — not clinical or stock-photo flat.
- Avoid clichéd staging; prefer authentic, aspirational moments.

### UI & Digital Design Language

Digital interfaces use the following consistent system:

- **Background:** Deep navy-to-charcoal radial gradient with ambient amber glow
- **Cards:** Frosted glass — `backdrop-filter: blur(22px)` + semi-transparent fill + subtle gold border
- **Accents:** Amber gold for borders, active states, labels, and CTA buttons
- **CTAs:** Gold gradient buttons (`#B8821E → #E8B84B`) with shimmer animation
- **Typography in UI:** Clean system sans-serif (Segoe UI / system-ui), spaced all-caps labels at 0.20em+
- **Motion:** Subtle — card entry animations, smooth panel slides, ambient pulse. Never distracting.
